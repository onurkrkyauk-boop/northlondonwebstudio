Our Story

A neighbourhood
café with soul.

Founded in 2019, Ember & Bean grew from a simple belief — that great coffee should feel like a moment to exhale. We source directly from small farms and roast in-house every week.

Our team has over 30 combined years of specialty coffee experience. Every drink is made with care, never rushed.
